Tap any days you want as guaranteed rest — the plan won't schedule anything on these.

Any fixed classes or sessions?

Add anything that's already in your week — pilates, spin, badminton, whatever. Tick "Account for" if you want the plan to schedule runs around it.

Any injuries or niggles we should know about?

We'll adjust the plan to protect you — fewer hard sessions, more walk/run, extra rest. This is not medical advice; see a physio if you're in active pain.
